## 📘 Project Overview
This project focuses on **Digital Literacy**, aiming to enhance digital awareness, technical skills, and professional communication. It is divided into **five practical modules**:

### **Task 1: Digital Literacy Infographic**
- Designed an infographic using **Canva** to explain:
  - What digital literacy is  
  - Useful digital tools for students  
  - Safe online practices  

### **Task 2: Digital Portfolio**
- Created professional profiles on **GitHub** and **LinkedIn**.  
- Added essential information to establish a strong digital presence.  

### **Task 3: Coding & Collaboration**
- Solved beginner-level coding problems on **HackerRank**.  
- Created a **Google Form quiz** for peer learning, including a response tracking sheet.  

### **Task 4: Email & Social Media Etiquette**
- Drafted professional emails for academic and workplace use.  
- Created a checklist for **responsible social media behavior**.  

### **Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness**
- Prepared a **phishing case study**.  
- Developed a checklist for **preventing online threats** and staying safe online.
